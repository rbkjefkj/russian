Have you tried to learn complete Russian before?

I mean the alphabet, grammar, real-world vocabulary, the case system, common used expressions, correct pronunciation and so on.

Yes?

Then I know you've probably wanted to bang your head against the wall!

Russian is an amazing language, I speak it with my girlfriend and friends all the time, but learning how to speak is not far from rocket science.

That's why I've made a simple beginner Russian course that will set you up to learn the basics of the Russian language well.

After you've learned a solid foundation of Russian vocabulary and grammar, you'll be motivated more than enough to continue on your own.

I've also translated Russian stories into English and added audio. It's the perfect way to get started reading Russian and immersing yourself into the language. Listen to the audio while you read, and pause to see the translation. This will allow your brain to make connections all on its own, while you're enjoying the story. Not to mention the numerous benefits for your Russian listening comprehension.

And that's just the beginning!

My mission is to help you learn Russian. That's why I publish outstanding content on the blog and create online lessons, guides, ebooks, checklists... for free!
